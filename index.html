Therapeutic Services

Rolfing® Structural Integration

A holistic system of soft tissue manipulation and movement education that organizes the whole body in gravity. Rolfing® addresses the body's connective tissue (fascia) to release, realign and balance the whole body, potentially resolving discomfort, reducing compensations, and alleviating pain.

Through a series of sessions, I work systematically to bring your body into better alignment, improving posture, flexibility, and overall well-being.

Orthopedic Massage Therapy

A medically-oriented form of massage that focuses on the treatment of pain and injury resulting from musculoskeletal imbalances, soft tissue dysfunction, and postoperative rehabilitation.

Using specific protocols, orthopedic massage addresses the underlying causes of pain by assessing and treating the affected muscles, tendons, ligaments, and fascia. This approach helps restore function, reduce pain, and improve mobility.

About Me & My Approach

Vince Milosevich is a Certified Rolfer® and Licensed Massage Therapist in Narberth, PA, specializing in lasting relief from chronic pain. Vince's journey into bodywork began out of necessity. After years of battling unexplained chronic neck and shoulder pain, and facing surgery without a clear cause, he discovered Rolfing® Structural Integration as a last resort. It was a revelation. Not only did he become pain-free without surgery, but he also found his calling to help others.

Today, Vince uniquely blends the whole-body approach of Certified Rolfing with targeted orthopedic massage techniques. He doesn't just focus on where it hurts; he works with your entire myofascial system to find and address the root cause of your pain. His treatments are informed by the latest pain science and are paired with practical self-care education, empowering you with the tools to maintain your results long-term. Vince's ultimate goal is to facilitate your healing so effectively that you graduate from needing his care, becoming his best referral source.

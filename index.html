Our Therapeutic Services

Rolfing® Structural Integration

A holistic system of soft tissue manipulation and movement education that organizes the whole body in gravity. Rolfing® addresses the body's connective tissue (fascia) to release, realign and balance the whole body, potentially resolving discomfort, reducing compensations, and alleviating pain.

Through a series of sessions, we work systematically to bring your body into better alignment, improving posture, flexibility, and overall well-being.

Orthopedic Massage Therapy

A medically-oriented form of massage that focuses on the treatment of pain and injury resulting from musculoskeletal imbalances, soft tissue dysfunction, and postoperative rehabilitation.

Using specific protocols, orthopedic massage addresses the underlying causes of pain by assessing and treating the affected muscles, tendons, ligaments, and fascia. This approach helps restore function, reduce pain, and improve mobility.

About Me & My Approach

With over 15 years of experience in therapeutic bodywork, I specialize in combining Rolfing® Structural Integration and Orthopedic Massage techniques to create personalized treatment plans for each client.

My journey began with my own recovery from a sports injury, which led me to explore various therapeutic modalities. I trained at the Rolf Institute of Structural Integration and completed advanced certifications in orthopedic massage and myofascial release techniques.

I believe in a holistic approach that addresses not just the symptoms but the root causes of discomfort and limitation. My practice focuses on helping clients develop a deeper connection with their bodies, enabling lasting change and improved well-being.

When I'm not working with clients, I enjoy hiking, practicing yoga, and continuing my education in the latest developments in manual therapy and movement science.
